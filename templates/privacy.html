Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Spendly respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We aim to keep things simple: Spendly is a personal expense tracker, and we only handle the data needed to make that work.

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information — such as your name, email address, and the password you create (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data — the transactions, categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions you choose to record.
  • Usage data — basic technical information such as your browser type, device, and pages visited, used to keep the service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect only to operate and improve Spendly. This includes displaying your expenses, processing the actions you take (like adding, editing, or deleting an entry), authenticating your account, and protecting the security of the service.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a database that is protected by reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. While no system is perfectly secure, we work to keep your information safe and to limit access to only what is necessary to provide the service.

You can delete your account and associated expense data at any time from your profile settings. Doing so will permanently remove your information from our active databases.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of third-party services to operate — for example, hosting providers and email delivery services. These providers receive only the information needed to perform their function and are expected to handle it securely.

We do not embed third-party advertising or analytics trackers in Spendly.

5. Your Choices

You can review and update your account information from your profile, and you can delete individual expense entries or your entire account at any time. If you have any questions about your data or want to make a request, please reach out using the contact details below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted indicates that you have read and accept the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at support@spendly.example. You can also review our Terms and Conditions for the full picture on using Spendly.
